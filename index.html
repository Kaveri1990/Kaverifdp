About Me

Kaveri Angappan Dedicated academician and researcher in the field of Computer Science, with a strong foundation in both teaching and applied research. My academic journey began with a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) from Gobi Arts and Science College, followed by a Master of Computer Applications (MCA) from Maharaja Engineering College. My passion for academia and research led me to pursue an M.Phil and subsequently a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Bharathiar University.


My areas of interest span across programming, computer networking, and data science, with a deep-seated passion for solving real-world problems through data-driven approaches. I have developed strong expertise in Python programming, which I frequently utilize in both teaching and research projects.


In the research domain, my core specialization lies in data mining. Over the years, I have contributed significantly to the academic community through numerous publications in reputed journals. My research work has been published in UGC-referred, Scopus-indexed, and SCI (Science Citation Index) journals, reflecting the quality and impact of my contributions.


As a lifelong learner and passionate educator, I strive to bridge the gap between theoretical computer science and its practical applications. I actively mentor students, collaborate on interdisciplinary research projects, and remain committed to advancing knowledge in the evolving landscape of computer science.
